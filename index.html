Introduction

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science, exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and philosophy. My research focuses on understanding the nature of machine intelligence and its alignment with human values.

Beyond code, I'm drawn to the philosophy of technology and cognitive science. I believe data is not merely fuel for computation, but a projection of human behavior in the digital realm. I'm committed to building intelligent systems that are not only efficient, but also interpretable and robust.
